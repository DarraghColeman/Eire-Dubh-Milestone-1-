Not all those who wander...

About


Forget Saint Brendan and Joe Biden. Never mind Maureen O'Hara and Barak O'Bama.

Éire Dubh looks at obscure figures and events of the Irish diaspora that maybe weren't much to write home about.


As we tend to laud the many successful and famous emigrants, and often attempting to lay claim on those with tenuous connections to our tiny island, many notable people and events abroad have gone under the radar. The phrase "Ah sure, we export all our best stuff!" isn't so true in some cases.

Nature or Nurture?



It would be unwise to avoid speaking about the external factors that would have played a large role in the making of notoriety. So, we have a look at the Irish diaspora today and events that caused such large exoduses. Check out the where, why and what lead to some infamous exports in our 'Places' page here.
